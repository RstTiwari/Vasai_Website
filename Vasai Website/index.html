One of the oldest City in the Maharashtra, Vasai is a mosaic of multicultural experiences. With a rich heritage and myriad attractions, the city is among the most popular tourist destinations in the Mumnai Region. It covers an area of 311. squrekillometere, extending from the snow-covered heights to the tropical rain forests of the south. ,Vasai stands apart from the rest of Maharastra, marked off as it is by mountains and the sea, which give a distinct geographical entity.
